<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in"><font style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, Serif; font-size: 10pt"><b>2.2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</b></font><b><a name="sp3_003"></a>Procedure
for Revolving Credit Borrowing. </b>The Borrower may borrow under the Revolving Credit Commitments during the Commitment Period on any
Business Day, provided that the Borrower shall give the Administrative Agent irrevocable notice (which notice must be received by the
Administrative Agent prior to (x)&nbsp;with respect to Eurodollar Loans, 10:00 A.M., New York City time, or (y)&nbsp;with respect to
ABR Loans, 1:00 P.M., New York City time: (a)&nbsp;three Business Days prior to the requested Borrowing Date, if all or any part of the
requested Revolving Credit Loans are to be initially Eurodollar Loans or (b)&nbsp;on the requested Borrowing Date, otherwise), in each
case specifying (i)&nbsp;the amount to be borrowed, (ii)&nbsp;the requested Borrowing Date, (iii)&nbsp;whether the borrowing is to be
of Eurodollar Loans, ABR Loans or a combination thereof and (iv)&nbsp;if the borrowing is to be entirely or partly of Eurodollar Loans,
the amount of such Type of Loan and the length of the initial Interest Period therefor. Each borrowing under the Revolving Credit Commitments
(other than a borrowing under <u>subsections 2.16</u> and <u>5.5</u>) shall be in an amount equal to (x)&nbsp;in the case of ABR Loans,
$5,000,000 or a whole multiple of $1,000,000 in excess thereof (or, if the Aggregate Available Revolving Credit Commitments are less
than $1,000,000, such lesser amount) and (y)&nbsp;in the case of Eurodollar Loans, $5,000,000 or a whole multiple of $1,000,000 in excess
thereof. Upon receipt of any such notice from the Borrower, the Administrative Agent shall promptly notify each Revolving Lender thereof.
Prior to (x)&nbsp;with respect to Eurodollar Loans, 11:00 A.M., New York City time, or (y)&nbsp;with respect to ABR Loans, 2:00 P.M.,
New York City time, on the Borrowing Date requested by the Borrower, each Revolving Lender will make an amount equal to its Funding Commitment
Percentage of the principal amount of the Revolving Credit Loans requested to be made on such Borrowing Date available to the Administrative
Agent for the account of the Borrower at the office of the Administrative Agent specified in writing by the Administrative Agent to the
Lenders in funds immediately available to the Administrative Agent. Except as otherwise provided in <u>subsection 2.16</u>, such borrowing
will then be made available to the Borrower by the Administrative Agent crediting the account of the Borrower with the aggregate of the
amounts made available to the Administrative Agent by the Revolving Lenders and in like funds as received by the Administrative Agent.</p>
